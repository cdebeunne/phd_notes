# Estimation of omnidirectional camera model from epipolar geometry

Author: Micusik

Year: 2004

Notes:
---
* Calibration of a spherical camera model with automatic correspondences based on epipolar constraint 

Camera model:
* projection equation:
$$
\alpha g(Au' + t) = PX \\
\alpha \in \mathbb{R}, \ g(u,v) = (u, v, f(u,v))
$$
